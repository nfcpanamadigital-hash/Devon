About Devon

A Lifetime Devoted
to the Piano

Devon Pruett brings over 30 years of hands-on piano expertise to every appointment — from intimate lessons with first-time students to precise tuning sessions for longstanding clients who have trusted him for decades. A University of Miami graduate and the inheritor of a family piano service tradition spanning 50 years, Devon has become one of Miami's most respected names in piano care and music education.

His students range from young children discovering the keyboard for the first time to adult learners returning to music after years away. Devon has a particular passion for working with students with special needs, incorporating Music Therapy principles to help them build lifelong skills alongside their musical growth.

As a multi-instrumentalist, Devon coaches piano, voice, trumpet, ukulele, and more — always meeting students where they are and guiding them toward where they want to be.

What Devon Offers

Services

Every service is delivered with 30 years of expertise, genuine care, and the precision that comes from a lifetime devoted to the instrument.

01

Private Piano
Instruction

Whether you're just beginning or refining advanced technique, Devon tailors every lesson to your goals, pace, and musical personality. Students of all ages — from young children to adult learners — develop genuine fundamentals, musical confidence, and a real love of playing. Classical, Pop, Jazz, and more. Lessons offered at Devon's studio or at your location.

02

Piano Tuning
& Repair

A well-tuned piano is the foundation of every great performance. Devon has been keeping Miami pianos in peak condition for over 30 years — carrying on a family tuning tradition that spans 50. He offers precise tuning, regulation, and repair services that restore tone, stability, and warmth to your instrument, whether it's a family upright or a concert grand.

03

Music
Engineering

With deep musical knowledge and technical proficiency, Devon provides music engineering support for recording sessions, production projects, and sound-forward creative work. He brings a working musician's ear to every technical decision — ensuring that what you capture reflects the quality and character of the performance.

04

Pre-Purchase Piano Inspection
& Buying Consultation

Thinking about buying a piano? Before you commit, have Devon evaluate the instrument. He assesses condition, tuning requirements, mechanical wear, playability, and overall value — giving you an honest, knowledgeable picture of exactly what you're buying. His guidance helps families, students, and institutions make confident, well-informed piano investments.
